Spica

A lightweight web-based tool for spatial and point cloud visualisation

What is Spica?

Spica is a browser-based tool designed to explore archaeological datasets through spatial visualisation and simple analytical layers.

It was developed primarily for research and teaching contexts, with a focus on transparency, reproducibility, and ease of use.

What problem does it address?

Archaeological datasets often combine spatial coordinates, stratigraphic information, and heterogeneous object categories.

Spica provides a way to visualise and compare these dimensions without requiring specialised GIS software or complex setup.

Main features

  • Load CSV-based archaeological datasets
  • Visualise point distributions in plan and section
  • Explore stratigraphic or categorical subsets
  • Overlay simple analytical surfaces (e.g. interpolation)
